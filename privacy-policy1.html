01 Introduction

Welcome to Fabricane ("we", "our", or "us"). Fabricane is a multi-platform e-commerce solution accessible at https://customer-eta-lovat.vercel.app that allows customers to shop across multiple stores and enables sellers to manage and promote their products.

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and protect your information when you use our platform, including our integration with third-party services such as Pinterest.

02 Information We Collect

Information You Provide

  • Name, email address, and contact details when you create an account
  • Billing and shipping address for order processing
  • Payment information (processed securely via third-party payment providers)
  • Store and product information if you register as a seller
  • Pinterest account credentials and board information when you connect your Pinterest account

Automatically Collected Information

  • Browser type, device information, and IP address
  • Pages visited, time spent, and actions taken on our platform
  • Cookies and similar tracking technologies

Information from Third-Party Services

When you connect your Pinterest account to Fabricane, we collect:

  • Pinterest user ID and profile information
  • Pinterest board names and IDs
  • Access tokens required to post and manage pins on your behalf

03 How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide and operate the Fabricane platform
  • Process orders and payments
  • Post and schedule Pinterest pins on your behalf when you authorize us to do so
  • Read your Pinterest boards to allow you to select where to publish content
  • Send transactional emails and notifications
  • Improve our platform and user experience
  • Comply with legal obligations

04 Pinterest API Usage

Fabricane uses the Pinterest API to provide content scheduling and posting features. Specifically, we use the Pinterest API to:

  • Create and publish pins to your Pinterest boards on your behalf
  • Read your Pinterest boards and profile to display them within our platform
  • Schedule pins for future publishing

We access your Pinterest data only with your explicit consent through Pinterest's OAuth authorization flow. You may revoke access at any time through your Pinterest account settings at pinterest.com/settings/security.

We do not sell, share, or use your Pinterest data for any purpose other than providing the features you have explicitly requested.

05 How We Share Your Information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share your information with:

  • Service providers who help us operate our platform (e.g., Vercel for hosting, payment processors)
  • Pinterest, as required to provide API-based features you have authorized
  • Law enforcement or regulatory authorities when required by law

All third-party service providers are required to maintain the confidentiality of your information.

06 Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide our services. Pinterest access tokens are stored securely and deleted when you disconnect your Pinterest account from our platform.

07 Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information, including:

  • Encrypted data transmission using HTTPS/TLS
  • Secure storage of access tokens and sensitive credentials
  • Regular security reviews of our platform

However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security of your data.

08 Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your personal information
  • Withdraw consent for Pinterest API access at any time
  • Opt out of marketing communications

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at the details below.

09 Cookies

We use cookies to maintain your session, remember your preferences, and analyze platform usage. You can disable cookies in your browser settings, though some features may not function properly without them.

10 Children's Privacy

Fabricane is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such information, please contact us immediately.

11 Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any significant changes by posting the updated policy on our website with a new effective date. Your continued use of Fabricane after changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
